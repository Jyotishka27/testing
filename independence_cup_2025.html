🏆 Independence Cup 2025

📅 Event Date:
24 August 2025

📍 Venue:
Turf XL

⏰ Time:
9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

👥 Organizers:
Debayan, Gourav & Jyotishka

👥 Captains:
Subhojit, Ritam, Monty, Subhamoy, Shubanjan & Akash Jr

⚽ Number of Teams:
6

Tournament Guidelines

Event Overview

  • Celebrating Independence Day with a spirited football tournament at Turf XL.
  • An opportunity to enjoy competitive play, sportsmanship, and camaraderie.

Important Notice

  • Organizers will not be liable for injuries, property loss, or death of any player.
  • Teams must carry their own medical kits and individual water bottles.

Field & Equipment

  • Field Size: 70 x 110 ft, clearly marked boundaries.
  • Ball: Regulation Size 5 football.

Team Composition

  • Minimum 7 players (including 1 goalkeeper).
  • Maximum 1 substitute allowed.
  • Players may represent only one team.
  • Maximum 6 players on the field at once; rolling substitutions.

Attire & Equipment

  • Teams must wear identical or similar-colored jerseys (pre-assigned, no changes).
  • Footwear: Spikes or joggers only.
  • No sharp accessories; nails must be trimmed.

Match Format

  • Match Duration: 16 minutes (8 per half) + 2 min half-time.
  • Final duration will be announced at venue.
  • Each player must play at least 15 minutes in group stage.
  • Strict timing; no postponements.
  • Teams with fewer than 6 players must play short.

Competition Structure

  • 2 groups of 3 teams each.
  • Round-robin format (2 matches per pairing).
  • Points: Win – 3, Draw – 1.
  • Tie-breakers: Goal Difference → Goals Scored → Coin Toss.
  • Knockouts: Semi-finals, 3rd place match, and Final.

Kick-off & Restarts

  • Coin toss decides ends and first kick-off.
  • After goals, restart from center spot by opponents.

Fouls & Misconduct

  • 2 yellows in one match → Red card for that match.
  • 2 yellows across matches → 12-min ban in next match.
  • 3 yellows in tournament → Ban from next match.
  • Direct red → Ban current + next match.
  • Referee’s decision is final.
  • Violent conduct may lead to disqualification.

Free Kicks, Corners & Throw-ins

  • Referee decides direct/indirect free kicks.
  • Indirect must touch another player.
  • 1m minimum distance for free kicks.
  • Standard corner and throw-in rules apply.

Tie-breakers (Knockout Stage)

  • 3 penalty kicks per team.
  • If tied → Sudden death penalties (2 rounds).
  • If still tied → Goal Difference → Goals Scored → Coin Toss.

Awards & Recognition

  • 🏆 Winner: Trophy + Gold Medals
  • 🥈 Runner-up: Trophy + Silver Medals
  • 🥉 Third Place: Bronze Medals
  • 🎯 Individual Awards: Top Goal Scorer, Best Goalkeeper, Playmaker, Best Defender, Man of the Match

Closing Ceremony

Award distribution begins post 5:00 PM.
